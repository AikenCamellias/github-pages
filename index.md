# Welcome to the Aiken Camellia Society

The Aiken Camellia Society is dedicated to promoting the appreciation, cultivation, and preservation of camellias in the Aiken, South Carolina area. Our members share a passion for these beautiful flowering plants and work together to educate the community about camellia care and cultivation.

## About Us

Camellias have a long and storied history in the Aiken area, thriving in our mild climate and enriching our gardens with their stunning fall, winter, and early spring blooms. The Aiken Camellia Society brings together enthusiasts of all experience levels to share knowledge, participate in shows, and celebrate these magnificent plants.

## Get Involved

Whether you're a seasoned camellia grower or just discovering these beautiful plants, we welcome you to join our community. Attend our meetings, participate in our events, and connect with fellow camellia enthusiasts.
