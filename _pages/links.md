A collection of resources for camellia enthusiasts.

- **[International Camellia Society](https://www.internationalcamellia.org/)** - Worldwide camellia organization
- **[Atlantic Coast Camellia Society](https://atlanticcoastcamelliasociety.org/)** - Regional camellia organization
